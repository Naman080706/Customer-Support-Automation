# ABC Technologies — Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General

**Q: What does ABC Technologies do?**
A: We provide cloud-based business management software covering invoicing, contacts, tasks, reporting, and automation for teams of all sizes.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A: Yes — a 14-day free trial is available for the Starter and Professional plans. No credit card is required.

## Account

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot password" on the login screen. A reset link will be emailed to you. Links expire after 1 hour.

**Q: How do I update my profile information?**
A: Go to Account Settings → Profile to update your name, email, and contact details.

**Q: How do I activate or deactivate my account?**
A: Account activation happens automatically after email verification. To deactivate, go to Account Settings → Status. Full account closure requires supervisor approval.

## Billing

**Q: How do I get an invoice?**
A: Invoices are emailed automatically and are also available under Account Settings → Billing → Invoices.

**Q: How do I request a refund?**
A: Submit a refund request from Account Settings → Billing, or ask support. Refunds are available within 30 days and require human supervisor approval.

**Q: My payment failed — what do I do?**
A: Verify your card details and available balance, then retry from Account Settings → Billing → Payment Methods. Contact Billing support if it keeps failing.

## Technical

**Q: Which browsers are supported?**
A: The latest versions of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.

**Q: What is the maximum file upload size?**
A: 50 MB per file. Larger files should be split or shared via a link.

**Q: How do I contact support?**
A: Email support@abctech.com, use in-app chat (Professional and Enterprise), or call the priority line (Enterprise).

## Sales

**Q: Can I upgrade my plan later?**
A: Yes, you can upgrade anytime from Account Settings → Billing → Change Plan. Upgrades are prorated.

**Q: Do you offer discounts?**
A: Annual billing saves 20%. Volume and non-profit discounts are available — contact Sales.
