# ABC Technologies — Company Policy Document

## 1. Refund Policy
- Customers may request a refund within **30 days** of the original purchase date.
- Annual subscriptions are eligible for a **prorated refund** for the unused period.
- Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable once the billing cycle has started, except in cases of proven service outage.
- **All refund requests must be reviewed and approved by a human supervisor** before any amount is returned.
- Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method within **5–7 business days**.

## 2. Subscription Cancellation Policy
- Customers may cancel their subscription at any time from **Account Settings → Billing → Cancel Plan**.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle; access continues until then.
- **Subscription cancellation requests require human supervisor approval** to ensure retention offers and data-export options are presented.

## 3. Account Closure Policy
- Account closure permanently deletes all customer data after a **14-day grace period**.
- **Account closure requests must be approved by a human supervisor** and cannot be automated.
- Customers are advised to export their data before closure.

## 4. Compensation & Service Credits
- Service credits or compensation may be offered for verified outages exceeding the **99.9% uptime SLA**.
- **All compensation requests require human supervisor approval.**

## 5. Escalation Policy
- Any request explicitly asking to escalate to management is routed to a **human supervisor**.
- High-risk requests (refunds, cancellations, account closure, compensation, escalation) are never auto-approved.

## 6. Data Privacy
- ABC Technologies complies with GDPR and SOC 2. Customer data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
- Personal data is never sold to third parties.

## 7. Support Hours
- Standard support: Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (customer local time).
- Priority/Enterprise support: 24×7.
