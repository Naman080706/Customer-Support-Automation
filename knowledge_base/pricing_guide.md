# ABC Technologies — Pricing Guide

ABC Technologies offers cloud-based business management software on a subscription basis. Three plans are available, billed monthly or annually. **Annual billing saves 20%.**

## Available Plans

### Starter Plan — $19 / user / month
- Up to 5 users
- Core business management modules (invoicing, contacts, tasks)
- 10 GB storage
- Email support (business hours)
- Ideal for freelancers and small teams.

### Professional Plan — $49 / user / month
- Up to 50 users
- Everything in Starter, plus automation, reporting dashboards, and integrations
- 100 GB storage
- Priority email + chat support
- Most popular plan for growing businesses.

### Enterprise Plan — Custom pricing (contact Sales)
- Unlimited users
- Everything in Professional, plus SSO/SAML, advanced security, audit logs
- Unlimited storage
- Dedicated account manager and 24×7 support
- Custom SLA up to 99.99% uptime.

## Add-ons
- Additional storage: $5 / 50 GB / month
- Advanced analytics module: $99 / month (flat)
- Premium onboarding: one-time $499

## Billing Details
- Accepted payment methods: major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfer (Enterprise).
- A **14-day free trial** is available for the Starter and Professional plans; no credit card required.
- Plans can be upgraded at any time; downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
- Annual plans are eligible for a prorated refund per the Company Policy (refunds require supervisor approval).
